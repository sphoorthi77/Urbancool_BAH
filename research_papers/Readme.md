# 🌎 Why This Repository?

UrbanCool is not just another AI project.

Every architectural component of UrbanCool has been inspired and validated using peer-reviewed scientific research from IEEE, remote sensing journals, climate science publications, and machine learning literature.

This repository serves as our **Literature Vault**, documenting the scientific knowledge that shaped the design of our platform.

# 🎯 Research Philosophy

> **UrbanCool transforms scientific research into actionable climate intelligence.**

Instead of only identifying **where cities are hot**, UrbanCool explains:

✔ Why the hotspot exists

✔ Which factors contribute the most

✔ Which intervention should be applied

✔ Where it should be implemented

✔ Expected cooling impact

✔ Cost-benefit trade-off

✔ Long-term climate resilience

Every recommendation is grounded in peer-reviewed scientific literature and designed to support evidence-based urban planning.
